ABOUT US

About Us – Tee & Tannery At Tee & Tannery, we believe that fashion is more than just what you wear—it's a reflection of your story, your style, and your journey. We combine the comfort and versatility of high-quality T-shirts with the timeless craftsmanship of premium leather goods, creating a unique blend of contemporary and classic fashion. Founded on a passion for design and craftsmanship, Tee & Tannery was born from the idea that the simplest garments can make the boldest statements. Whether it’s a perfectly tailored tee or a meticulously crafted leather jacket, our products are designed for individuals who value style, quality, and authenticity. Our commitment to excellence drives us to source the finest materials and pay attention to every detail, from the stitching on our tees to the finish on our leather. We stand behind every piece we create, ensuring that you can feel as good as you look, knowing your style is built to last. Tee & Tannery is more than a brand—it’s a lifestyle. It's for the dreamers, the doers, and the trendsetters who aren’t afraid to blend modern flair with timeless tradition. Join us on this journey, and let us help you tell your story—one stitch, one hide, and one tee at a time.

Welcome to Tee & Tannery—where your style is crafted with care.
